about us

Mercedes is known for the highest build quality and use of premium materials. Natural wood, high-quality leather, aluminum and other durable and luxurious materials are used in the interior of the cars.

Mercedes-Benz traditionally introduces advanced technologies, including active and passive safety systems, the MBUX intelligent multimedia system with voice control and artificial intelligence support.

Mercedes actively works to improve safety levels and is often an innovator in this area. Safety systems such as Pre-Safe, Attention Assist and Blind Spot Assist help prevent accidents and minimize risks.
